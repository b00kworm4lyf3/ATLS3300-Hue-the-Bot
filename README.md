"HUE THE BOT"

Need a designer friend or a break from your work without having to use your phone? Hue is a robot desk friend who will help you design, keep you engaged, and provide you company. Do you need to use a color from real life in your design? With a color sensor and button to initiate a color scan, he is able to read the color of physical objects and announce the hex code on his belly. For stimulation? That hex code gets translated into RGB values that will be displayed on his cheeks and his face, and with an accelerometer, he will respond to your interaction. Tilt his body!
